Bangladesh

Specifications

  • Local Name: বাংলাদেশ (Bangladesh)
  • Proportion: 3:5
  • Name of the Flag: বাংলাদেশের জাতীয় পতাকা (National Flag of Bangladesh)
  • Adopted: January 17, 1972

Symbolism

    Colors:

  • Green: Youthfulness, vitality, and the lush landscape of the nation
  • Red: The rising sun and the blood of those who died for independence

    • Shapes / Symbols:

  • Red circle slightly shifted left: Movement toward progress and equality

Meaning / Special Display

  • The flag is raised prominently on Independence Day and Victory Day as a tribute to the martyrs of the Liberation War.
  • Displayed at educational institutions and government offices to signify national unity and remembrance.

Description

  • The flag embodies Bangladesh’s struggle, resilience, and pride in its identity as a nation shaped by sacrifice.
  • Citizens view it as a reminder of their heritage, cultural strength, and commitment to building a prosperous future.
