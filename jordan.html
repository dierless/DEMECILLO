Jordan

Specifications

  • Local Name: الأردن‎ (Al-Urdun)
  • Proportion: 1:2
  • Name of the Flag: علم الأردن (Flag of Jordan)
  • Adopted: April 18, 1928

Symbolism

    Colors:

  • Black: The Abbasid Caliphate
  • White: The Umayyad Caliphate
  • Green: The Fatimid Caliphate
  • Red Triangle: The Hashemite dynasty and the Arab Revolt

    • Shapes / Symbols:

  • Seven-pointed white star: Represents the seven verses of Al-Fatiha and unity of the Arab people

Meaning / Special Display

  • Commonly displayed during Independence Day celebrations on May 25, symbolizing national unity and pride.
  • The seven-pointed star is often highlighted in government events as a reminder of Hashemite leadership.

Description

  • The Jordanian flag represents the country's deep Arab heritage, Hashemite leadership, and Islamic tradition.
  • It reflects Jordan’s identity as a stable and culturally significant nation in the Middle East.
