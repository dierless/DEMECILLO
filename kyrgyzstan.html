Kyrgyzstan

Specifications

  • Local Name: Кыргызстан (Kyrgyzstan)
  • Proportion: 3:5
  • Name of the Flag: Кыргызстандын желеги (Flag of Kyrgyzstan)
  • Adopted: March 3, 1992

Symbolism

    Colors:

  • Red: Bravery, valor, and the historical spirit of the Kyrgyz people
  • Yellow (Sun): Peace, prosperity, and the warmth of the homeland

    • Shapes / Symbols:

  • 40-rayed sun: The legendary 40 clans united under Manas, the national hero
  • Tunduk (yurt crown): Home, unity, and the traditional nomadic identity

Meaning / Special Display

  • The flag is commonly displayed at yurts, cultural festivals, and horseback games celebrating Kyrgyz nomadic traditions.
  • It appears prominently during the World Nomad Games, symbolizing heritage and the revival of traditional sports.

Description

  • The flag reflects Kyrgyzstan’s deep cultural roots centered on unity, hospitality, and the nomadic way of life.
  • It is regarded as a symbol of national pride, resilience, and the connection between modern Kyrgyz society and its ancient past.
