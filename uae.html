United Arab Emirates

Specifications

  • Local Name: الإمارات العربية المتحدة (Al-Imārāt Al-‘Arabīyah Al-Muttaḥidah)
  • Proportion: 1:2
  • Name of the Flag: علم دولة الإمارات العربية المتحدة (Flag of the United Arab Emirates)
  • Adopted: December 2, 1971

Symbolism

    Colors:

  • Red: Courage, bravery, and unity
  • Green: Fertility and prosperity
  • White: Peace and honesty
  • Black: Strength of mind

Meaning / Special Display

  • Raised on National Day and important government events, representing unity of the seven emirates.

Description

  • The flag represents the UAE’s unity, national pride, and federation of emirates.
  • For its citizens, it symbolizes loyalty, patriotism, and collective progress.
