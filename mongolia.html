Mongolia

Specifications

  • Local Name: Монгол Улс (Mongol Uls)
  • Proportion: 1:2
  • Name of the Flag: Монгол Улсын төрийн далбаа (State Flag of Mongolia)
  • Adopted: February 12, 1992

Symbolism

    Colors:

  • Red: Progress and prosperity
  • Blue: Eternal sky and freedom

    • Shapes / Symbols:

  • Yellow Soyombo symbol on the hoist side: Represents fire, sun, moon, earth, water, and yin-yang; national independence and strength
  • Vertical triband of red-blue-red

Meaning / Special Display

  • The Soyombo is a sacred national emblem and is only displayed on official state and ceremonial occasions.
  • The flag is raised during national holidays, such as Naadam, reflecting unity and cultural pride.

Description

  • The flag embodies Mongolia’s cultural heritage, independence, and connection to nature and the eternal sky.
  • For citizens, it signifies freedom, strength, and national unity.
