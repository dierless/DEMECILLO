Maps of Asia

Discover the geographical layout of Asia and its place in the world through these maps.

Map of the World

This world map provides a global context, showing Asia's vast expanse and its connections to other continents. Asia, the largest continent, covers about 30% of Earth's land area and is home to over 4.6 billion people, representing diverse cultures, economies, and landscapes from the icy tundras of Siberia to the tropical islands of Southeast Asia.

Map of Asia

The map of Asia highlights the continent's boundaries, major countries, and key geographical features. Spanning from the Ural Mountains in the west to the Pacific Ocean in the east, and from the Arctic Ocean in the north to the Indian Ocean in the south, Asia includes iconic landmarks like the Himalayas, the Gobi Desert, and bustling metropolises such as Tokyo and Mumbai. It encompasses 48 countries, each with unique flags and histories.

Map of Asian Provinces/Regions

This map illustrates the various provinces or sub-regions of Asia, dividing the continent into areas such as East Asia, Southeast Asia, South Asia, Central Asia, West Asia, and North Asia. These divisions help understand the cultural, political, and geographical diversity within Asia, from the mountainous regions of Central Asia to the archipelagos of Southeast Asia.
