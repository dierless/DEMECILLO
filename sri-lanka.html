Sri Lanka

Specifications

  • Local Name: ශ්‍රී ලංකා (Śrī Laṃkā)
  • Proportion: 1:2
  • Name of the Flag: Lion Flag of Sri Lanka (Sinhala: ශ්‍රී ලාංකීය සිංහ සෙරපිය; Śrī Lānkīya Siṃha Serapiya)
  • Adopted: December 17, 1978

Symbolism

    Colors:

  • Maroon: Sinhalese ethnicity
  • Orange stripe: Tamil ethnicity
  • Green stripe: Muslim ethnicity
  • Yellow border: Unity and Buddhism

    • Shapes / Symbols:

  • Lion holding a sword: Courage and strength of the nation
  • Four leaves in corners: Buddhism’s four virtues (Metta, Karuna, Mudita, Upekkha)

Meaning / Special Display

  • Displayed on national holidays and ceremonial events to honor ethnic harmony and national pride.

Description

  • Reflects Sri Lanka’s multi-ethnic composition, Buddhist heritage, and national courage.
  • For citizens, the lion symbolizes bravery and unity among diverse communities.
