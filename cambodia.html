Cambodia

Specifications

  • Local Name: កម្ពុជា (Kampuchea)
  • Proportion: 2:3
  • Name of the Flag: The Flag of Cambodia
  • Adopted: June 30, 1993

Symbolism

    Colors:

  • Blue: Liberty, cooperation, and brotherhood
  • Red: Bravery and valor
  • White: Integrity and the nation’s heritage

    • Shapes / Symbols:

  • Angkor Wat: Symbolizes Cambodia’s spiritual heritage, national identity, and pride in its ancient civilization

Meaning / Special Display

  • The flag is one of the few in the world to feature a building — the iconic Angkor Wat.
  • It represents national unity and devotion to the country’s cultural and religious foundations.

Description

  • The flag consists of three horizontal stripes: blue on the top and bottom, red in the center, with a detailed depiction of Angkor Wat in white.
  • For citizens, the flag reflects pride in Cambodia’s ancient empire and resilience through its history.
