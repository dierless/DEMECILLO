Kazakhstan

Specifications

  • Local Name: Қазақстан (Qazaqstan)
  • Proportion: 1:2
  • Name of the Flag: Қазақстанның Мемлекеттік Туы (State Flag of Kazakhstan)
  • Adopted: June 4, 1992

Symbolism

    Colors:

  • Sky Blue: Peace, unity, and the vast Kazakh steppe

    • Shapes / Symbols:

  • Sun with 32 rays: Energy, warmth, and life
  • Golden Steppe Eagle: Freedom, independence, and the aspirations of the Kazakh people
  • National Ornament (Koshkar-Muiz): Traditional Kazakh cultural identity

Meaning / Special Display

  • Displayed prominently on Constitution Day and Independence Day, symbolizing national pride and sovereignty.
  • The flag is commonly raised at cultural festivals to highlight Kazakhstan’s nomadic and steppe heritage.

Description

  • The flag expresses Kazakhstan’s harmony, cultural continuity, and the strength of its independence.
  • The sun and eagle reflect the nation’s forward-looking spirit and the importance of freedom in Kazakh identity.
