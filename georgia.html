Georgia

Specifications

  • Local Name: საქართველო (Sakartvelo)
  • Proportion: 2:3
  • Name of the Flag: Five Cross Flag (or Sakartvelo's flag)
  • Adopted: January 14, 2004

Symbolism

    Colors:

  • White: Purity, peace, and innocence
  • Red: Courage, bravery, and the fight for freedom

    • Shapes / Symbols:

  • Large central cross: Represents St. George, the patron saint of Georgia
  • Four smaller crosses: Symbolize Christianity and the nation's strong religious heritage

Meaning / Special Display

  • The Five-Cross design is one of the world’s oldest Christian flags, dating back to medieval Georgian kingdoms.
  • It is displayed on national holidays, historic celebrations, and during religious ceremonies.

Description

  • The flag features a white background with a large red cross dividing the field and four smaller red Bolnur-Katskhuri crosses in each quadrant.
  • For Georgian citizens, it represents deep Christian identity, unity, strength, and national pride.
