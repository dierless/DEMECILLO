Iraq

Specifications

  • Local Name: العراق (Al-ʿIrāq)
  • Proportion: 2:3
  • Name of the Flag: علم العراق (ʿAlam al-ʿIrāq)
  • Adopted: January 22, 2008

Symbolism

    Colors:

  • Red: The struggles and sacrifices of the Iraqi people
  • White: Peace and a hopeful future
  • Black: The historical battles and endurance of the nation

    • Shapes / Symbols:

  • Takbīr (الله أكبر): “Allahu Akbar” written in green Kufic script, symbolizing faith and national identity

Meaning / Special Display

  • Displayed during national remembrance days to honor martyrs and periods of struggle.
  • Raised at government institutions and public events to reinforce unity across Iraq’s diverse communities.

Description

  • The flag reflects Iraq’s complex history, cultural depth, and the resilience of its people.
  • It serves as a reminder of national unity, faith, and the shared determination to rebuild the country.
