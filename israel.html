Israel

Specifications

  • Local Name: יִשְׂרָאֵל (Yisra'el)
  • Proportion: 8:11
  • Name of the Flag: דֶּגֶל יִשְׂרָאֵל (Degel Yisra'el)
  • Adopted: October 28, 1948

Symbolism

    Colors:

  • Blue: Divine favor, spirituality, and devotion
  • White: Peace, purity, and integrity

    • Shapes / Symbols:

  • Star of David (Magen David): Jewish identity and heritage
  • Two horizontal blue stripes: Reminiscent of the traditional Jewish prayer shawl (tallit)

Meaning / Special Display

  • Flown on national holidays and religious occasions, reflecting Jewish heritage and the state’s unity.
  • Displayed at government buildings and international events as a symbol of Israel’s identity.

Description

  • The flag embodies Israel’s historical and religious significance as the Jewish homeland.
  • It represents national pride, spiritual devotion, and the unity of its citizens.
