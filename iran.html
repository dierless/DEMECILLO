Iran

Specifications

  • Local Name: ایران (Īrān)
  • Proportion: 4:7
  • Name of the Flag: پرچم ایران (Parcham-e Irān)
  • Adopted: July 29, 1980

Symbolism

    Colors:

  • Green: Islam, growth, and hope
  • White: Peace and honesty
  • Red: Courage, sacrifice, and national strength

    • Shapes / Symbols:

  • Central emblem: A stylized form of “Allah,” symbolizing faith and identity
  • Takbīr script repeated 22 times along green and red bands: Represents the date of the Iranian Revolution (22 Bahman)

Meaning / Special Display

  • Used prominently during religious events and national celebrations to signify unity and revolutionary heritage.
  • Displayed at government institutions as a mark of national sovereignty and faith.

Description

  • The flag reflects Iran’s identity rooted in faith, tradition, and national resilience.
  • It serves as a symbol of cultural pride and the enduring spirit of the Iranian people.
